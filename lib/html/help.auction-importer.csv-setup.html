CSV Setup

Setup your CSV with the following columns: LotNum, Title, Description, LowEst, HighEst, Realized, Highlight, Tags, and iGavelLotNum. Here's an example:

LotNum Title Description LowEst HighEst Realized Highlight Tags iGavelLotNum
1 Lot 1: 3 Chinese Snuff Bottles inc. Cicada 1st item: Chinese bronze cicada from snuff bottle with... 500 600 324.50 bronze
2 Lot: 2 Chinese Archaic Bronze Censer, Jade Final Chinese Archaic Bronze Censer or Ding, likely Zhou Dyn... 900 1100 20650 true chinese,jade

Notes

  • When exporting a spreadsheet from Excel, choose "Windows Comma Separated (.csv)" as the file format.
  • iGavel Online Auctions: Add a link to any item's corresponding iGavel auction page by adding column called iGavelLotNum (case-sensitive) to your CSV.
